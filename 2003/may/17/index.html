➠ May 17, 2003

Saving the universe… One screen at a time

Screensavers on OSX have long been decidedly lacking. So much so that I even spent a while hacking around trying to get XScreenSaver to compile, just so I could have some pretty eye-candy.

Then the other day, Dan linked to a rather snazzy Matrix-esque display. The saver is notable not only for being rather attractive, but also the sheer amount of effort the author put in to emulating precisely the graphics from the Matrix. It’s really rather frighteningly obsessive and sweet.

Crap Log links to a couple more, which manage to effortlessly combine being exceptionally geeky and mathematical, with cool-to-watch. Hopefully now we’re going to see some funky “Screen Effects” (as Apple bemusingly refer to them) come to light.
