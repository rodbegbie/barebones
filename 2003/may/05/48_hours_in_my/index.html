48 Hours in my life

This weekend, I’ve:

  • Seen Phone Booth – Capsule review: I thought it was Kiefer-packed silliness and really rather enjoyable, Joy thought it was crap and preachy.
  • Completely caught up with the episodes of 24 and parted repartitioning my Linux box and installing ReiserFS. I feel immense amounts of pride in the fact that I managed this without blowing anything up and/or losing 3 years worth of email.
  • Purchased a Roomba, which is now sat in the kitchen while its battery charges. 12 bleeding hours of charging before we can set it loose. Gah! Impatience is gnawing at my soul.

This is probably the most relaxing, and yet productive, weekends I’ve had in months. It’s a shame I still haven’t unpacked.
