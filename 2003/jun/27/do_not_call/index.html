➠ June 27, 2003

Do Not Call

About bloody time. The National Do Not Call Registry has launched their registration site. Now you can tell telemarketers to piss off in advance.

The site is, unsurprisingly, somewhat swamped at the moment, but rather brilliantly, all numbers on the Massachusetts Do Not Call List Registry (which I’ve been on since Day 1) will be automatically added to the federal list. Throw your anti-telemarketing counterscript away, and start answering the phone once more!
