I Only Have Eyes for You

Yesterday, I took part in a very fun workshop at ImprovBoston run by Josh & Tamra, a pair of improvising puppeteers from New York. By far, the highlight for everyone involved was being handed a pair of ping-pong balls on a bit of elastic which created the effect of a very basic puppet:

Now I’ve started eyeing up (pun not entirely intended) the puppets on sale at Arms Length Productions. I’m meant to saving up for the wedding, but I’m finding myself trying desperately to justify dropping $150 on a puppet.
