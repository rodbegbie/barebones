My Maciversary

➠ January 13, 2003

My Maciversary

Clearing out some old mail folders, I realised that it’s exactly one year since I bought my first-ever piece of Apple hardware: A 5Gb iPod.

It really was the proverbial Trojan Horse. Since then, I’ve purchased a second-hand G3 Blue & White, upgraded to a 20Gb iPod, then splashed out a cool $2k on a super-shiny, sleek and sexy G4.

I watch Steve Jobs’s keynotes with an element of excitement. I ooh and aah at his demonstrations, and then when I get my hands on the items in question, I ooh and aah again (Seriously—I wanted to wake Joy up last night to show her how incredible One Click Enhance is!).

And then this weekend, I sold off the old G3. The purchaser was a guy who’d received an iPod as a Christmas present. And so the cycle starts again.
