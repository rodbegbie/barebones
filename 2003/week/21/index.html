➠ May 31, 2003

T-Shirt Whore

Threadless.com screams my name with freakish accuracy.

Threadless allows anyone to upload a shirt design. The inertnet community votes and comments on these designs. Then once a month, they pick a handful of the top-scorers and turn them into available-to-buy limited-run honest-to-goodness t-shirts.

I’m a total t-shirt whore. I freely admit that I have far more shirts than I could ever wear, and a (sadly) ever-increasing number of them no longer fit due to my (sadly) ever-increasing belly. But still I hold on to them, in the vague hope that I will one day be slender enough to squeeze my frame into that Size “M” Idler snail shirt I bought when I was still a (relatively) scrawny student.

Brilliant stuff.
