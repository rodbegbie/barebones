Wa-hey!

Found out this morning that “The Play What I Wrote” (a tribute to top British comedy duo Morecambe & Wise) is being transferred to Broadway in a couple of months.

I’d love to see it, although I’m concerned as to the level of cultural dilution that’ll have taken place to sell the show to US audiences.

Perhaps I’ll just hold out until “Cannon & Ball – The Musical” is written.

(It’s worth noting that my suggestion to Joy that we play “Bring Me Sunshine” at the end of our wedding ceremony was rejected without a second thought.)
