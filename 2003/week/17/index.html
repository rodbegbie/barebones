No, seriously — Curse you, Steve

Just dropped $30 on downloads from the Apple music store. Couple of albums and a handful of random tracks. My lord, they’ve designed the software well. Easy to search, easy to browse, easy to navigate, and far too damned easy to buy from.

Curse you, Steve Jobs!

Of course I want a new iPod. The 20Gb I currently have is straining at the seams, barely able to contain my ever-expanding MP3 collection. I see they’ve fixed the crappy remote-control connector too.

The music store looks pretty smart too. You actually feel like you own the download—You can burn it, load it onto your iPod, use it as a soundtrack for iMovie—Pretty much the only thing you can’t do is share it on Kazaa.

The less said about the advertising campaign the better, though. shudder.
