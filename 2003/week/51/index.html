eventSherpa

eventSherpa—The first iCal rip-off I’ve seen for Windows.

As with most such things, the UI misses out on the simplicity that makes iCal so great, but the web front-end is considerably more useful than Apple’s, erm, complete lack of one.

(Oh, and apropos of very little, apparently “Sherpa Tensing” is the brandname for a Swiss sunscreen. The mind boggles.)
