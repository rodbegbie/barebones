This Brain was made for Walking

I used a SportBrain in 2001 when they first came out, and thought it was great. In summary, it’s an internet-enabled pedometer. It counts the steps you take each day, then displays them in a stylish graph on their website, encouraging you to walk more.

The original company went pop along with the inertnet “bubble” (despite the fact that the company was profitable! Investors were just scared of anyone with a website at that time), but it resurrected itself last month.

Visit the site and login using email “visitors@groovymother.com” and the password “rodwalk”, you can see how I’ve been doing. 6.3 miles yesterday!
