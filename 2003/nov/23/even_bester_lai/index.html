Even Bester Laid Plans

I’m a smart traveller. Not for me the being suckered into buying Traveller’s Cheques, or wasting money on commission at the Bureau de Change. Nope, I have a British bank account, so I wired our spending money for the trip to it last week.

This evening, I looked out my British ATM card from its Safe Hiding Place™. Lawks a mercy, it was there! Who’dathunkit?

And then I looked at it. And realised that it expired last month.

Arse.

A flawless plan, with only one major fucking flaw in it.

So our first morning in London will be spent trekking across the city to one of only two sodding branches of Bank of Scotland, in order to withdraw cash. Bah.
