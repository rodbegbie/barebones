Syndirella — Off to the Ball

Yole is planning on giving up developing Syndirella. That’s a tremendous shame, since Syndirella has been the best news aggregator I’ve seen for Windows.

But I totally understand it. When you’re developing something, there’s that magical point where it stops being fun and starts being work.

All the cool, shiny, brain-twisting problems have been solved; Now you’re down to the dull, annoying, banging-your-head-against-the-wall stupid bugfixes and tweaks, which generally take thirty minutes to find, and thirty seconds to fix.

All the best, Yole. I may well take you up on that offer of CVS write access.
