Clobbering Commentspam

Amongst the 199 messages in my Inbox upon getting home (not counting all the mailing list stuff filtered away, and the 427 spams caught by SpamAssassin) were a bunch of emails from MovableType telling me comments had been posted to my blog. Frustratingly, most of these were spammers trying to abuse blogs to garner precious PageRank.

I spent a wee while this morning pondering on ways to block the spammybastards. Then I loaded Bloglines and found someone had already done all the hard work for me. MT-Blacklist—Essential if you’re running MT and being clobbered by commentspam too.
