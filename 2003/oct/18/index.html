Saturday Night at the Movies. and Friday Night. and Saturday Afternoon. and…

After being really quite busy for the last few weeks, and since our evening schedule for next week has helpfully been cleared, Joy & I decided it was time to catch up on a load of movie releases that passed us by recently. So this weekend is going to be spent practically living in the local multiplex.

Last night was School of Rock and Runaway Jury, to be followed by Kill Bill, Mystic River and Intolerable Cruelty today.

And over the next week, we hope to hit Lost in Translation and Pirates of the Caribbean, the latter of which is currently playing at the second-run cinema down the street.

I’m going to be sick of Junior Mints by this time next week, I’m sure.
