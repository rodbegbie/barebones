Hi Jack

➠ October 30, 2003

Hi Jack

Megawickedawesome app of the day: Audio Hijack. Truly a thing of beauty. It syphons off the audio from applications like Real Player and stores them on your hard drive.

Sure, there’ve been hacks to do this on other OSes for ages, but none do it as stylishly as Audio Hijack. It includes a scheduler to record from “the radio” when you command, and can automagically compress it to an AAC file and add it to your iTunes library.

This morning, I awoke to find a rerun of Just a Minute ready to sync onto my iPod for the journey to work. Hurrah! Well worth the $16 registration.
