Microserfs

I’ve been re-reading Microserfs (Hey, I’ve got to read something while I wait for the new Lemony Snicket book)

The first time I read it, I was still in second year at University, so it didn’t really “speak” to me. But now, being a mid-twenties guy who has been working in the US techy industry for four years, it really strikes a chord. And I just love the following quote (which was my email signature for a while)

"It's like male geeks don't know how to deal with a real life woman, so they just assume it's a user interface problem. Not their fault. They're just waiting for the next version to come out - something more 'user friendly'."
