Lies, Damned Lies and the RIAA

RIAA up to more badness
"We want to hit fans with the message that downloading music illegally is, as Britney Spears explains, the same as going into a CD store and stealing the CD," said Hilary Rosen of the Recording Industry Association Of America (RIAA).

Except, erm… that it isn’t.

If you steal a CD, the record store has already paid the distributor, who has already paid the record label, who has theoretically already paid the artist. You’re stealing from the record store owner.

If you download music, no-one gets paid anything… but the record store still has a copy of the CD which it can sell for a profit.

The RIAA would prefer it if everyone shoplifted CDs. Hell, they’d still get paid.

People that talk in metaphors ought to shampoo my crotch. Britney, that means you.
