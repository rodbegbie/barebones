Calibration

Apple have finally released their calendar package iCal for download. It’s definitely a nice wee package, but for my money, the kewlest feature is the “Publish & Subscribe” system, whereby you can publish your calendar to a website and allow others to view it against their own calendar.

When I was at SAPE, this was a vital part of Exchange, now it’s available for the home user.

Apple are even kind enough to publish some examples for you to subscribe against. Very neat.
