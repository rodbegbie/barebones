Piet Depsi

I’m spending my time on the T getting funny looks from strangers, due to chuckling out loud to myself. No, not due to mental health problems. Instead, I’m busy reading the staggeringly funny Letters from a Nut series of crank letters. They’re collections of unbearably well-crafted letters to companies from a totally insane author, along with their sober, sincere or litigious responses.

This page gives you an idea of what to expect. I think this may be my first "drop-off" for BookCrossing.
