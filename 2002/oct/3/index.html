➠ October 3, 2002

Peter Sissons

The Mercury News has an article about “newsreaders”. I’ve been using (and loving) NetNewsWire on my Mac for the last month or so, and it’s truly a fantastic wee bit of kit. But this article turned up Feedreader which is a valiant attempt at a PC equivalent. Still a bit rough around the edges, but lovely nonetheless.

(And as an aside, who the hell decided to call these new XML-parsing-HTML-scrapers “newsreaders”, thereby overloading a word that’s been quite happily used for 20 years to describe NNTP-chattering Usenet clients? Gah. Progress, eh?)
