Recycle or Die

An explanation for the cryptic stickers on the T.

Apparently, if you get your bag/limb/pet trapped in the doors on older trains, it won’t reopen for you. Which is pretty much a life-or-death situation, so hurrah for the MBTA for producing such clear, understandable signage to protect their customers.
