Froogle

Google have launched a new online-store searching system, the punningly-named Froogle.

I guess I had high hopes for something engineered by those bright sparks, but it still has one of the same major problems that most other of these engines have: It fails to take punctuation in a model number into account. For example a search for HBH-30 should hopefully also give the same results as a search for HBH30.

Come on (Fr|G)oogle!
