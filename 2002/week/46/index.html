The Office

I spent yesterday watching my DVD of The Office. I’d heard so much about it that I bought the DVD without ever having seen an episode! And I am pleasantly surprised by just how good it is. I can definitely identify traits in the characters which remind me of ex co-workers (particularly from my time working for Comet)

And, as is traditional for any British sitcom with an ounce of success, a US version is now in the planning stages. It would have to be a cable station like HBO which took the series, because it would never play out on a broadcast network. Nowhere near cheery and jokey enough for the canned-laughter loving masses.
