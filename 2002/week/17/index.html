I am nobody. *sob*

I have officially disappeared from the inertnet. The latest Google refresh has somehow removed begbie.com from their index. So now a search for my name just turns up old posts I’ve made on various mailing lists (and, shame of shame, slashdot).

It’s especially frustrating since some funky new search engines are popping up (such as the new BBC Search or Alexa Site Info) which are based on Google’s data.

This must have been what it felt like to have been one of the people who was purged by Stalin when he was doing his Stalinesque Purge of whatever it was that Stalin purged.
