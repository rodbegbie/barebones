MP3s are so old skool

Today, I received a rather charming reintroduction to the joys of the vinyl records. I’d ordered the new Lemon Jelly single from their website, and I have to admit, I got a little bit of a chill as I removed the inner sleeve from the outer, and the record from the inner sleeve, and placed it on the turntable and started it playing.

There’s just something so wonderfully tactile about the experience. The careful thumb-and-forefinger manouvre to get the record out without getting your smudgy prints on it. The clunkiness of the arm moving over the start playing. The feel of the disc as you flip it over to play the B-side. Just lovely.

And the music’s pretty decent too.
