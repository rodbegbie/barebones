Doo Happens

Saw the new Scooby Doo trailer the other night—And it really leaves me feeling uncomfortable.

It’s like the producers didn’t know whether to do a Very Brady Pisstake or a proper for-the-kids flick. So they’ve done a half-assed version of both. It really doesn’t bode well.

On Monday, May 6, 2002, Jaynee commented:

I'll have to disagree on the Scooby comments. The trailer made me want to see it more. I had serious doubts about the movie when I first heard it was being done. But as soon as I saw the trailer I was impressed with two things:

1) Matthew Lillard doing a SPOT ON impression of Casey Kasem's Shaggy voice.
2) the obvious (and hilarious) typecasting of Freddie Prinze Jr as the vacuous Fred and Sarah Michelle Gellar in the Buffy-esque Daphne.

I'm probably going to go see it, having seen the trailer. Now let's hope it IS as good as the too-funny "Brady Bunch Movie", which I loved!
