The Theoretical Truth?

This fluttered by in my links a couple of months ago, but it bears repeating now.

If you’re at all a fan of the excellent, excellent Series of Unfortunate Events books (and you bloody well should be, cause they’re chuffin’ ACE!), and are, like me, anxiously awaiting the twelfth installment, you owe it to yourself to rummage around at The Nameless Novel. In amongst the strange quotes, unexpected recipes and questionable facts are plenty of clues as to what’s coming next. (And if you get stuck, the Wikipedia entry has the answers).

Quite impressively, with three weeks to go til publication, the book’s title still remains an enigma wrapped in a mystery wrapped in a marmoset.
