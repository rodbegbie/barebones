CD Purchase: Mogwai - Young Team (Remastered)

The classic Mogwai album remastered with an extra disc of rarities and live tracks.

I first bought Young Team in 1997 (since I was a fan of the Glasgow lo-fi scene of the time), but I completely failed to "get" Mogwai (where the hell are the tunes and lyrics‽), and gave the CD away to someone. After seeing them live downstairs at The Middle East a few years later, (This was just after they'd sold "Blur are Shite" t-shirts at T in the Park, so I was wearing a homemade "Mogwai are Pish" one), it completely clicked, and I now love them with a passion unbridled.

If you don't already have a copy, splash out on this two disc set.
