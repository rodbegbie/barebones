➠ August 6, 2009

Netflix: Freedom & Responsibility Culture

Really interesting presentation deck leaked(?) from Netflix, covering their workplace culture. Some incredibly smart and inspiring stuff. Apparently, they let go with a generous severance package anyone who fails the question: “Which of my people,  if they told me  they were leaving in two months  for a similar job at a peer company,  would I fight hard to keep at Netflix?”
