Chimpy Chompies

One of the prime factors in British Anti-American sentiments has been the rebranding of chocolate bar & sweets to match their US brethren.

!http://www.groovymother.com/images/marathon.jpg 207×73 (Marathon)!

One of the first of these, and until the Opal Fruits/Starburst debacle, by far the most painful to Brits was the renaming of the venerable Marathon to the nonsensical “Snickers”. Which, frankly, makes the US launch of Snickers Marathon(SNICKERS® MARATHON™ Long Lasting Energy Bar) bars all the more insulting. Piss all over our heritage, whydontcha.
