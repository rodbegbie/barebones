➠ March 9, 2004

House!

What started last week as idle “we should buy a house” chatter, evolved over the weekend into hardcore phoning-round-realtors and calling banks to find out about mortages.

Because, you know, since we got the wedding over and done with, we haven’t had a back-breakingly complicated high-stress life-changing project, and, frankly, we were missing it; all this free time was just killing us.

Task for tonight: Spend some time reading House Buying for Dummies and Jeremy Zawodny’s blogged house-buying experiences.
