Civic Unrest

Looks like my pedestrian days are over (Although in fairness, I’ve been using Zipcar a fair bit over the last couple of months).

In order to make it out to my new job, I’ll be required to drive out to the beautiful city of Woburn each day, which has necessitated the purchase of a car. Hopefully, if everything goes to plan, by this time tomorrow, I’ll be the owner of a shiny almost-new Honda Civic, and will be driving past the only Krispy Kreme in Massachusetts on a daily basis.

This can’t be good.
