A Greyer Shade of Pale

After reading about the legal furore surrounding The Grey Album—a hip-hop remash of the vocals from Jay-Z’s “The Black Album” with samples taken entirely from The Beatles’ “White Album”—I, of course, had to rush out and download a copy.

And it’s an absolute corker of an “album”. Hearing familiar (and, since they’re from the White Album, incredibly varied) sounds, under some great rapping, is disconcertingly great. It’s grown on me with every listen. Heavily recommended.
