Re-caffeinated

If you run into me in the next few weeks, forgive me if I seem more jittery than usual. I’ve just picked up a Senseo Single Serve Coffee Machine, and I frickin’ love it! It’s a cunningly well-designed piece of hardware which brews up a mug of quality frothy coffee in less than a minute. I only opened it two hours ago, and I’m already on my third mug (although I switched, rather sensibly I feel, to Decaf for the third).

If you want one for yourself, there’s a $10 mail-in rebate available until the end of the month. Just go easy if you want to sleep any time soon.
