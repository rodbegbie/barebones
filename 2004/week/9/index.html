Gawd Bless Newbury Comics

Another reason why I love Newbury Comics…

I was in their Woburn store today, which is pretty much automobile-accessible-only (and just off a couple of Interstates)

I purchased two CDs and was asked by the clerk if I wanted him to open one for me.

What a great service! He ripped off the cellophane and removed the sticky security seal, and handed me the unsheathed case, saving me from having to do so in the car, thereby adding some shreds of wispy plastic to the accumulating litter on the passenger seat.

Mr. Newbury Comics, for your services to reduced car-clutter, I salute you.

Routeword

I’ve been meaning to post about RouteWord for a while now. It’s a fun daily word puzzle—Follow the letters around the patch to make a word. My reason for posting this today? I created today’s puzzle.

That said, what really appeals to me about the puzzle is its origin as a tool for graph visualization. Takes me back to my Uni days looking at Turing machines and finite-state automata.

The submission applet is rather ace. Plug in words and see them turned into Routewords (Also fun: try entering palindromes like “madamimadam” or “amanaplanacanalpanama”). It’s strangely hypnotic.
