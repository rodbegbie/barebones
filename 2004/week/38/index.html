Like A Champagne UKNova

I posted UKNova to the links list a month or two ago, but I want to promote it more now. It’s a fabulous resource for Brit ex-pats, or just fans of British TV.

Currently being circulated on the site are torrents of the forgotten-but-fantastic sitcom How Do You Want Me?, how-the-mighty-have-fallen piss-poor Angus-Deayton-hosted Bognor Or Bust, excellent Stephen-Fry-hosted-smug-fest QI and the always-entertaining-annoyance-baiting of Room 101(Room 101 (TV series) – Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia).

Fire up your torrents, and enjoy!
