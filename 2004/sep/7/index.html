What the well-scheduled TiVo will be wearing this Fall

I’ve just spent a very pleasurable hour or so, making my annual trawl through the Entertainment Weekly Fall TV Preview.

Joey was already set on our TiVo, and I’ve been looking forward to JJ Abrams’s new series, Lost, for a while. But the new series that intrigues me most is House, a new drama starring the fabulous Hugh Laurie. I hadn’t heard of the show until tonight, but I suppose that by the time it premiers, following Fox’s incessant promotions during the World Series, I’ll be sick to death of it.
