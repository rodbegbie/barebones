➠ January 14, 2004

The name’s Foothill… Gargoyle C. Foothill

It’s a common spammer trick to make mail look like it’s come from one of your friends, by cunningly populating the From line with common first names. I have to wonder, though, what this spammer had been smoking when they coded this random name generator…

UPDATE: Another one today from “Tallyhoed L. Spoonbills”. OK, that’s my new pseudonym on message boards from now on!
