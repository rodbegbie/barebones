43 Things

Ooh. Looks like 43 Things is out of beta. (Or at least it’s available to the public)

I’ve been playing with it, and it’s fun. Start by building a list of up to 43 things that you want to do: Some short-term, some long-term. Some acheivable, some fanciful. You can see my goals to get an idea.

Then once you have the list written, you can start thinking of ways to implement them, using the site to log your progress, encourage others to help you, and generally make progress. Just writing down “Visit Chicago” reminded me that I had airmiles available, so in May, Mrs B and I are heading to “The Windy Apple”.

Could be a good way to track your new years resolutions!
