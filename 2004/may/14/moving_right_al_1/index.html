Moving Right Along

After a little over a year at our current apartment, moving day comes around once again. And rather than do something useful like, you know, pack some of the shite still littering the place, I thought I’d blog about it, under the pretence of this announcement:

There’ll be some breakage as the PC that theoretically “hosts” this site gets disconnected from the “net” and thrown on the back of a truck for a few hours.

As if you care.

UPDATE: (9.26am) Well, after two hours of sleep and an all-night packingfest that is almost vaguely resembling half-way done, I can honestly say, with my hand on my heart, that I fucking hate moving.

If I have my way, we’re staying in the new house until the 30-year mortgage is paid off.
